Evil Santa

Play the Game

Overview

Get ready for some holiday mayhem in Evil Santa, the ultimate stress-relieving Christmas game! Once a jolly gift-giver, Santa has gone rogue, trading presents for pranks and spreading chaos instead of cheer. But don’t worry—you have a chance to stop his mischief!

Smash, bash, and teach this troublemaking Santa a lesson before he ruins Christmas. The game is designed to be interactive and user-friendly, making it accessible for all skill levels. Let the festive smackdown begin!

How To Play

  • Step 1: Use your mouse to click on Santa Claus.
  • Step 2: Click on Santa to select different ways to attack him using the weapons above.
  • Step 3: Choose from bare hands, a gun, a speeding train, or even Christmas tree decorations!
  • Step 4: Keep hitting Santa to collect coins, which you can use to buy new weapons in the shop.
  • Bonus: Monitor Santa’s health at the bottom of the screen and keep track of your level progression.
